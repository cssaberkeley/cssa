General Member Vs. Internal Member

General Member

Open to everyone – join anytime!

  • Access to all CSSA general meetings and events
  • Connect with fellow Cognitive Science students
  • Participate in workshops and skill-building sessions
  • Network with alumni and industry professionals
  • Stay updated through the mailing list and CSSA Slack
Join General Mailing List

Internal Member

Application-based – open every semester

  • Help organize and host the annual CogSci Conference
  • Priority access to professor dinners and lab tours
  • Exclusive social events and team bonding activities
  • Build leadership and professional development skills
  • Join specialized committees to contribute to club operations

Internal Committees

Design

Composed of four subdivisions: club marketing, conference marketing, merchandise, and newsletter.

  • Produces all visuals for social media, recruitment, and merchandise
  • Creates in-house writing and design for the monthly newsletter: The Neural Dispatch

Media

Plans overall marketing campaigns for general events, recruitment, and conference.

  • Manages social media presence, tabling initiatives, and promotional outreach
  • Maintains active engagement on Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, and more

Tech

Manages the club website and conference Eventbrite platform.

  • Sets up all hardware including cameras and microphones, oversees online streaming
  • Undertakes additional projects such as the CogSci coursemap

Finance

Oversees financial operations for all club endeavors.

  • Books conference venues and secures donations from UC Berkeley departments and external donors
  • Allocates funding for merchandise, retreats, socials, fundraising events, and collaborations

Social

Plans social events and conducts outreach initiatives.

  • Collaborates with other campus organizations
  • Organizes social gatherings and team bonding activities

Professional Development & Outreach

Coordinates guest speakers and external partnerships.

  • Recruits potential speakers including professors and industry professionals for CCSC
  • Reviews research abstract submissions and executes bimonthly professional development programming
