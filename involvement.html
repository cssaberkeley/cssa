CSSA Alumni

CSSA Alumni

Where are they now? CSSA has put together a map of Cal cog sci alumi locations stretching from the Bay Area to around the world. CSSA alumni will be highlighted in PURPLE. This project aims to connect and maintain contact with alumni of the cogsci program here at UC Berkeley and hope to create a network that can benefit both students and alumni. If you’re an alumni and would like to get involved, subscribe to the emailing list and/or join the map!
