    Thank you for your interest in the 12th annual California Cognitive Science Conference (CCSC), originally scheduled for Saturday, April 25, 2020. In light of the pandemic, the CSSA at Berkeley has decided to cancel the conference for the current academic year. We hope you understand our decision in light of this unusual situation. We will be in touch in the coming months with further details, but don’t hesitate to reach out with any questions! We hope you all stay safe and healthy and look forward to seeing you in the fall! - CSSA at Berkeley
